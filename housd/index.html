About us

Our community moved in together in the Summer of 2018 and purchased our beautiful home in Grass Valley in 2021. We are seeking an individual or couple to join our household.

We are a two couples and Chuck (four adults plus one cat!) in our mid 30s to early 40s and are a heart-centered/mindful group of creatives, engineers, healers, and dancers. We love eating/hanging together in the kitchen, cozying up for movie nights, working out together, and having little house getaways just as much as we enjoy rejuvenating, low-key, introvert time. We all have our own lives and schedules, but also make time for our house community.

Some of Our Community Values
  • Welcoming differing beliefs, ideas, and backgrounds.
  • Minimizing our footprint on planet Earth, exploring ways and choosing options whenever possible that reduce our impact and promote health of our bodies and our land. This may look like abstaining from chemicals and toxins, buying bulk, being mindful of energy use, etc.
  • Caring for and stewarding the land that we live on.
  • Queer members and LGBTQIA+ and all lineages stoked.
  • Creativity! We welcome outside the box thinking and multi-faceted means of self-expression.
  • Participation in house cleanliness and organization.
  • Open and direct communication, healthy boundary setting, willingness to engage and meet each other if disagreements arise.
  • Willingness to share our hearts and be comfortable with emotional vulnerability.
  • Body and sex positivity / Nudity welcome.
  • Attunement to each other and context in shared living spaces
